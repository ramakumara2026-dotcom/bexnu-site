Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

BEXNU ("we", "our", "the app") is a positive-news reader. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices — in plain language.

What we collect

  • Anonymous user ID. When you first open BEXNU, we create an anonymous identifier (via Firebase Authentication) to sync your preferences. We do not require your name, email, or phone number to use the app.
  • Preferences stored on your device. Your selected countries, interest categories, dark mode setting, notification preferences, and reading streak are stored locally on your phone.
  • Usage analytics. We use Firebase Analytics to understand which features are used, so we can improve the app. This data is aggregated and not tied to your identity in any personal way.
  • Push notification token. If you enable notifications, your device registers with Firebase Cloud Messaging so we can deliver good-news alerts and your daily quote.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not require an email address, phone number, or real name.
  • We do not read your contacts, photos, or other apps.

Advertising

At Present BEXNU does not display third-party advertisements and does not sell your data to advertisers or any other party. There is no ad-tracking of any kind in this app.

How article content works

BEXNU's backend automatically crawls publicly available news sources and uses AI to identify matching stories as per BEXNU's vision. This process analyzes article text, not your personal data. We display short excerpts with attribution and a link to the original publisher — we do not claim ownership of third-party news content.

Third-party services we use

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, analytics, push notifications, crash reporting
  • Railway — hosts our backend server and database
  • Anthropic — processes article text (not personal user data) to classify positivity

Children's privacy

BEXNU is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices

  • You can disable notifications anytime in Settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences from your device.
  • To request deletion of any server-side interaction data tied to your anonymous ID, email us at support@bexnu.ai.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@bexnu.ai.
